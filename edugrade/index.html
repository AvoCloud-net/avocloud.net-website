WHAT IS EDUGRADE?

A digital notebook for teachers

EduGrade is a free web application that helps teachers manage students, their grades, and attendance in one organized place. Everything in one spot - no paper piles, no complicated spreadsheets.

The app runs entirely in the browser: on the computer in the staff room, on a tablet in class, or at home. One account is all you need - and your data is always stored securely encrypted.

WHO IS IT FOR?

EduGrade is made for …

Teachers

Whether you teach one class or multiple subjects: record grades, track attendance, and get reports at the click of a button - without spreadsheets.

Schools & Departments

EduGrade can be self-hosted on your school's own servers - full control over all data, with no dependency on external services.

Privacy-conscious users

All data is stored encrypted - not even the server operator can view student data. Ideal for GDPR-compliant schools.

SECURITY & PRIVACY

Student data, well protected

All data is stored encrypted

All student data is secured with strong encryption - right at the moment of saving. This means: even if someone were to gain unauthorized access to the server, they cannot read the data. Only those who know the correct password have access.

The principle is called Zero-Knowledge: not even the operator of the app can view the stored data. The data belongs solely to the teacher.

Strong encryption Zero-Knowledge Password-protected only

Automatic sign-out

After one hour of inactivity, you are automatically signed out. Ideal for computers in the classroom or shared devices.

1-hour session Auto sign-out

Secure grade sharing

When students need to view their grades, they receive a personal PIN. The link expires automatically - no risk of misuse.

6-digit PIN Time-limited Secured link
