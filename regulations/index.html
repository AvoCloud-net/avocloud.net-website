DATA PROTECTION REGULATIONS

Our Privacy Policy

Last update: 21.08.2025

In the points and sub-points below you will find all the necessary information regarding privacy. It explains how we handle the data, what we store and more.

Users may contact us by e-mail to request a copy of the information we have on file or to have it deleted.

Data Security

All data is stored on our own servers, accessible only to members of our administration team. Our use of self-developed or open source programs ensures transparency and accountability.

We don't use any kind of tracking in our systems. Some pages, like the hosting dashboard or our Baxi dashboard, use cookies to save the login session.

Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have certain rights:

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You may ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Restriction: You may ask us to stop processing all or some of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@avocloud.net. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

Baxi

Baxi is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We only process and store the minimal information required to provide specific services enabled on each guild.

The following data may be processed:

  • User IDs
  • Guild IDs
  • Message IDs and content (in specific contexts)
  • Attached files (in specific contexts)

What we store and why:

  • Chat Filter Logs: If the chat filter deletes a message, a log entry is created (username, content, guild name, IDs). Accessible to moderators via an access link. All processing is performed exclusively on our own servers. No data ever leaves the Avocloud network.
  • Global Chat Data: For messages in global chat channels, we store the Message ID, Guild ID, and Author ID. Attached images are uploaded to our servers with a random link.
  • Web Dashboard Logs: Actions taken via the dashboard are logged and visible to the guild owner for transparency.

Baxi operates 100% independently on our own infrastructure. We do not share or sell any data with third parties.

SkyFire

We try to safeguard your privacy with our SkyFire hosting services. However, to ensure compliance with our hosting policies, we reserve the right to conduct random audits of customer servers.

The following team members have access to customer servers:

  • Red_wolf2467 – redwolf2467@avocloud.net

Minecraft

We run several Minecraft servers, all operated via Paper. All standard information is stored on our servers. We use custom anti-cheat software called "IAC" (illegal anticheat).

AvoFilter – Plugin

Our Minecraft plugin "AvoFilter" sends every message to our self-developed SafeText backend. No data or requests are stored. The backend keeps data in working memory only until the request is complete, then deletes it.

SafeText Server

  • We do not store any information about the content sent. All data is deleted after the request is processed.
  • Communication between API client is secured with HTTPS & HSTS.
  • The code is available on GitHub for transparency.

We try to develop as much as possible ourselves, and only use open source software where possible. We at avocloud.net do not give away, sell or share any of the data we process or collect.

DISCORD RELATED RULES

Discord Related Rules (DRR)

Last update: 15.12.2024

The following rules must be adhered to on our Discord server. Failure to comply may result in a temporary or permanent suspension. These rules also apply to our global chat operated by Baxi.

Peaceful Interaction

  • Treat all members with respect and kindness.
  • No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind.
  • Avoid personal attacks or inflammatory comments.

No Spamming

  • Spamming messages, images, or links is prohibited.
  • Please refrain from excessively repeating the same message in a short time.
  • Avoid mass tagging others without reason.

NSFW and Offensive Content

  • Explicit or NSFW content is strictly prohibited.
  • No sharing of inappropriate or offensive material in any form.
  • Respect everyone's boundaries and preferences.

No Self-Promotion or Advertising

  • Do not promote channels, servers, services or products without permission from the server team.
  • Passing on referral links or advertising other servers is not permitted.

Respect Privacy

  • Do not share anyone's personal information without their consent.
  • Respect others' privacy and do not engage in doxxing or any harmful behavior.

Follow Channel-Specific Guidelines

  • Each channel has a specific topic, so please keep messages relevant to the channel's purpose.
  • If you're unsure about where to post, ask a mod for guidance.

Listen to Staff

  • Respect the decisions and instructions of server moderators and admins.
  • If you have concerns about a decision, address it politely and privately with the staff.

No Cheating or Exploiting

  • Do not use cheats, hacks, or exploits that disrupt the server's activities.
  • Engaging in unfair practices or cheating is grounds for a ban.

German or English only

  • Please use only English or German on our entire server, whether in voice or text channels.
  • Let's keep the community fun and welcoming for everyone!

No Trolling or Disruption

  • Avoid trolling, disrupting conversations, or creating unnecessary chaos.
  • Let's keep the community fun and welcoming for everyone!

Enforcement and Appeals

Our moderation team follows these guidelines:

  • Minor infractions typically result in a warning.
  • Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent restrictions.
  • You may appeal moderation decisions by contacting support@avocloud.net with relevant details.
  • Appeals will be reviewed by a team member not involved in the original decision.
