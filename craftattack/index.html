WHAT IS CRAFT ATTACK?

What is Craft Attack?

Craft Attack is a community Minecraft survival format originally popularized by German YouTubers and streamers. The Avocloud.net Craft Attack server is an open Craft Attack community — a public, free-to-join CraftAttack project where everyone can participate, not just invited content creators.

Season 2 — The Ashes of Aerlon — runs on a custom-generated fantasy world with Java & Bedrock crossplay, clans, jobs, an economy, opt-in PvP, voice chat, and a live BlueMap. Server address: craftattack.avocloud.net.
