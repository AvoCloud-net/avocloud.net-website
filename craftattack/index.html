DIE ASCHE VON AERLON

A World Reborn from Ash

Aerlon was once a great kingdom, powerful, radiant, full of magic and life. What exactly happened, nobody remembers anymore. All that remained was ash: overgrown ruins, silent forests, forgotten paths.

Now new travelers come to the land. Not as heroes or heirs, simply as the first to return. Some seek treasure. Some seek a new beginning. The land is open. What you make of it is up to you.

Custom World – Aerlon

Aerlon stretches across dense forests where stone arches of an older age still loom between the trees, over vast arid plains backed by towering cliffs, through overgrown plazas lined with ancient columns, past forgotten settlements behind weathered wooden gates, and down into dark underground chambers where old structures still stand.

Our Goal

Build a world together, as a community. Whether you're a streamer, a YouTuber, or just someone who wants to have fun and explore: everyone is welcome. No gates, no elites. Just people who want to create something together.

NEW IN SEASON 2

Season 2 keeps everything that worked in Season 1 and adds new systems based on community feedback and lessons learned.

Clan System

Form groups, build together, and declare wars (opt-in).

Clans have their own chat (/cc), bank, home point, claim territory, and shared chest. Ally with other clans or declare war. Both require mutual consent.

New in S2

Coin Economy

Earn coins, expand your territory.

Start with 500 Coins. Earn more through mob kills, playtime bonuses, Jobs, and trading. Coins fund claim expansion beyond the free 500 blocks.

New in S2

PvP Toggle System

Opt-in PvP with 24h cooldown.

Use /pvp on to enable. Turning it back off has a 24h cooldown. New players get 48h auto-protection. Spawn is always PvP-free.

SERVER RULES

Official Server Rules v3.0

Core Principle: These rules exist so everyone has fun. When in doubt: respect, fairness, common sense. Ask staff if unsure.
  • 1. No Griefing: Don't destroy, damage, or alter other players' builds, farms, Redstone, or claimed areas.
    • Applies to claims AND actively used unclaimed areas
    • Unclaimed pure wilderness may be modified normally
    • Accidental damage? Report immediately and it will be restored
    Punishment:
    • Minor (1–5 blocks): 1-day timeout + restoration
    • Moderate (6–20 blocks): 3-day timeout + restoration
    • Major (21+) / malicious: 7-day ban; repeat → permanent ban
  • 2. No Stealing: No access to chests, inventories, or protected areas without permission.
    • All containers: Chests, Shulkers, Barrels, Hoppers, Dispensers, Furnaces
    • "Borrowing" without permission = theft
    • Auto-Hopper pointed at others' containers = immediate violation
    • Graves are protected (see Rule 6)
    Punishment:
    • Low value: 2-day timeout + item return
    • High value / rare items: 7-day ban; repeat → permanent
  • 3. Respectful Conduct: No insults, discrimination, hate speech, harassment, threats, or doxxing, in-game and on Discord.
    • Zero tolerance: racism, sexism, homo-/transphobia, slurs
    • Repeated contact after "stop" = harassment
    • Trolling RP scenes = harassment
    Punishment:
    • Minor: 1h timeout + warning
    • Repeated: 1–3 day timeout
    • Severe (hate speech): 7-day ban minimum
    • Extreme (doxxing, threats): permanent + IP ban
  • 4. Claims & Clans: Protect your builds with the claim system. Respect others' claims. Follow clan rules.
    • First 500 blocks² free; beyond that costs Coins (via Homestead)
    • No overlapping claims; 10-block minimum distance
    • 60 days inactivity → auto-unclaim (80% Coin refund if voluntarily unclaimed)
    • Clan founding: 3 players + 5,000 Coins; leader is responsible for members
    • Alliances and Wars require mutual consent
    Punishment: Claim/clan abuse → 3-day ban + dissolution
  • 5. Consensual PvP: PvP requires /pvp on from both players. Spawn is always safe.
    • Default: /pvp off for all players
    • /pvp on → off: 24h cooldown
    • Newbie protection: 48h auto-off for new players
    • Spawn (500 block radius): always PvP-free
    • Combat Tag: 15 sec, no logout, teleport, or PvP toggle
    • Combat-Log = treated as death
    • Duel System: arena-based, no loot loss, available for all
    • Clan Wars: opt-in, 7-day auto-end; no base destruction, grave rules still apply
    Punishment:
    • Unauthorized PvP: 1h timeout
    • Traps / spawn camping: 3-day timeout
    • Status abuse: 1-day timeout; repeat → 7-day ban
  • 6. Absolute Grave Protection: Graves are untouchable for 15 minutes after death. No exceptions.
    • Grave = death chest at death spot; protection radius 3 blocks
    • Only the owner can open during the 15-minute window
    • After 15 min: items drop and are free for anyone
    • Applies in ALL contexts: PvP, Clan Wars, RP, "they deserved it"
    • No trapping (lava, cactus, pistons, mob spawners) or blocking access
    Punishment:
    • Accidental (reported immediately): warning + restoration
    • Intentional: 3-day ban minimum
    • Malicious (trapping): 7-day ban; repeat → permanent
  • 7. No Cheats or Unfair Mods: No hack clients, X-Ray, auto-farmers, entity radar, or coordinate tools.
    • Forbidden: Cheat clients (Wurst, Sigma, Impact), combat hacks (aimbot, reach, killaura), movement hacks (fly, speed, nofall), X-Ray + ore-highlight packs, entity radar, auto-farming without input, coordinate finders, chunk pre-gen tools
    • Minimaps allowed: Surface mapping, own position, own waypoints
    • Minimaps forbidden: Cave mode, radar, entity tracking
    Punishment:
    • 1st offense: 3-day ban + mod removal verification
    • 2nd offense: 14-day ban
    • 3rd offense / distributing cheats: permanent + IP ban
  • 8. No Lag Machines: Builds and farms must not degrade server performance.
    • Staff checks via /tps and profiling
    • Large Redstone farms are fine as long as TPS stays stable
    • Optimization chance is given before any penalty
    Punishment:
    • Minor: warning + 1h fix time
    • Persistent: 6h timeout + required fix
    • Crash risk: 3-day ban + staff removes the build
  • 9. Roleplay is Voluntary: RP is allowed, but nobody is forced. No coercion, no trolling of RP scenes.
    • Toggle with /rp on/off; active RP players show [RP] prefix
    • RP players may not pressure others to participate
    • Non-RP players don't disrupt active RP scenes
    • OOC mark: ((Text)) or [OOC]
    • OOC stop signal applies ALWAYS: ((stop)) or "OOC Stop" in voice chat
    • Sensitive topics (violence, death, trauma, discrimination) require explicit OOC consent
    • Character death / item loss only with mutual OOC agreement
    • RP never overrides server rules. RP violations are double-punished
    Punishment:
    • 1st offense: warning
    • 2nd offense: 1-day timeout
    • Ignoring OOC stop / RP griefing: 3–7 day ban
Advertising: External content (Discord servers, YouTube channels, other Minecraft servers, shops) may not be promoted in chat, on signs, in books, or in player names. Community-related content is usually fine, ask staff if unsure. Abuse: timeout up to permanent ban.
Reporting: Via Discord ticket at avocloud.net/discord ↗
Required: screenshots with coordinates/timestamps OR video. Response time: <24h.

3-Strike System: Strike 1 = temporary ban · Strike 2 = longer ban (min. 7 days) · Strike 3 = permanent ban.
Instant perma-ban for: hate speech, doxxing, mass griefing, cheat distribution.
Strikes reset after 6 months without violations. Accidents reported immediately may not count.

Appeals: 7-day window via Discord ticket. Reviewed by 2+ uninvolved staff. Decision provided in writing.

Community First: These rules exist so everyone has fun. Education before punishment. If you see a violation: don't retaliate, document it, report via Discord ticket, trust the process.

SERVER MONITORING & LOGGING

Transparency in Moderation

To ensure a safe and fair environment for all players, we run several monitoring and logging systems:

  • Chat Logging: All in-game chat messages are logged and forwarded to a staff-only Discord channel for moderation purposes.
  • Player Analytics: The "Plan – Player Analytics" plugin monitors server performance and assists with moderation decisions.
  • CoreProtect Logging: All block placements, destructions, item drops/pickups, and container access are logged. Used exclusively for grief investigations and rollbacks.
  • Public AntiCheat Logging: All AntiCheat detections and resulting punishments are logged in a public Discord channel for full transparency.
Privacy Commitment: All logged data is used solely for server moderation, performance optimization, and community protection. We do not share personal gameplay data with third parties.
