Features — Craft Attack Community Server Season 2

Full feature list of the open Avocloud Craft Attack community server, Season 2: The Ashes of Aerlon. Season 2 keeps everything that worked in Season 1 and adds new systems based on community feedback.

New in Season 2

Clan System

Clans have their own chat (/cc), bank, home point, claim territory, and shared chest. Ally with other clans or declare war — both require mutual consent. Roles: Leader · Manager · Member. Founding: 5,000 Coins.

New in S2

Coin Economy

Start with 500 Coins. Earn more through mob kills, playtime bonuses, jobs, and trading. Coins fund claim expansion beyond the free 500 blocks².

New in S2

Jobs System

Pick one of four jobs and earn Coins through your normal playstyle: Hunter (slay mobs), Miner (mine ores), Farmer (harvest crops), Woodcutter (chop trees). Use /jobs browse. Only one job at a time.

New in S2JobsReborn

PvP Toggle System

Use /pvp on to enable. Disabling has a 24h cooldown. New players get 48h auto-protection. Spawn is always PvP-free.

New in S2PvPManager

Custom World – Aerlon

Dense forests with ancient stone arches, towering cliffs, overgrown plazas, forgotten settlements, dark underground chambers. Procedurally generated with a custom world generator, fully pre-generated for lag-free exploration. Live BlueMap web map.

New in S2BlueMap

RP Status System

Toggle with /rp on. Active RP players show [RP] in chat. Roleplay is always optional — nobody is forced.

New in S2

Flexible Region Claims

Homestead replaces chunk-based claims. Define any region shape with 75+ permission flags. First 500 blocks² free, then costs Coins.

Upgraded in S2Homestead

Returning Features

Everything that made Season 1 great is back.

Java & Bedrock Crossplay

Play together regardless of platform: Java or Bedrock (consoles, mobile, Windows 10/11). Geyser + Floodgate bridge both editions seamlessly.

Returning

One-Time Elytra at Spawn

Receive an Elytra at spawn to explore the world. Used once, then consumed. Keeps the start fair while still giving a special boost.

Returning

Chest & Block Protection

Secure chests, doors, and Redstone with a simple right-click. Nobody accesses your containers without explicit permission.

Returning

Voice Chat with 3D Audio

Spatial voice chat in-game. Hear exactly which direction other players are speaking from. (Java Edition only.)

Returning

Transparent Moderation

CoreProtect logs all block actions. AntiCheat results in a public Discord channel. BlueMap web map lets everyone explore live.

Returning

Whitelist System

Whitelist via Discord account link. No application form — connect Discord and Minecraft via the Baxi bot to get auto-whitelisted.

Returning
