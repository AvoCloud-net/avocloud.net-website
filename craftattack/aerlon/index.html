Aerlon — The Custom World of Craft Attack Season 2

Aerlon is the procedurally generated fantasy world that hosts Craft Attack Season 2 — The Ashes of Aerlon on the open Avocloud Craft Attack community server. Overgrown ruins, towering cliffs, ancient plazas, floating islands, and forgotten settlements form a single connected map, fully pre-generated for lag-free exploration from day one.

A World Reborn from Ash

Aerlon was once a great kingdom — powerful, radiant, full of magic and life. What exactly happened, nobody remembers. Only ash remained: overgrown ruins, silent forests, forgotten paths. Now new travelers come to the land. Not as heroes or heirs, simply as the first to return. Some seek treasure. Some seek a new beginning. The land is open. What you make of it is up to you.

Custom World

Aerlon stretches across dense forests where stone arches of an older age still loom between the trees, over vast arid plains backed by towering cliffs, through overgrown plazas lined with ancient columns, past forgotten settlements behind weathered wooden gates, and down into dark underground chambers where old structures still stand.

Our Goal

Build a world together, as a community. Whether you're a streamer, a YouTuber, or just someone who wants to have fun and explore: everyone is welcome. No gates, no elites. Just people who want to create something together.
