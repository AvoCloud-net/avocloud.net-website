Clans, Jobs & Economy — Craft Attack Community Server

The open Avocloud Craft Attack community server in Season 2 — The Ashes of Aerlon ships a full set of community systems: clans, jobs, a coin economy, flexible Homestead claims, and opt-in PvP. Everything here is free; coins are earned, not bought.

Clan System

Found a clan, share a base, declare wars or forge alliances. Each clan gets its own chat (/cc), bank balance, home point, claim area, and shared chest. Roles: Leader · Manager · Member. Alliances and Clan Wars require mutual consent — wars are opt-in, auto-end after 7 days, do not allow base destruction, and grave protection still applies.

Coin Economy

Every player starts with 500 coins. Earn more through mob kills, playtime bonuses, jobs, and trading with other players. Coins fund Homestead claim expansion beyond the free 500 blocks², clan founding, alliances, and player-driven shops.

Jobs System

Pick one of four jobs and earn coins through your normal playstyle — mining, building, farming, hunting. Only one job at a time. Browse with /jobs browse. Jobs are entirely optional but the most reliable income stream for funding clan and claim growth.

Homestead Claims

Flexible region claims, not just chunks — claim any shape with 75+ permission flags. First 500 blocks² are free; beyond that costs coins. Auto-unclaim only triggers when upkeep can't be paid (insufficient balance on /money). Default rule keeps some distance to neighboring claims; directly adjacent claims allowed only with explicit consent of the affected claim holder.

Opt-in PvP

PvP is off by default. Enable with /pvp on; disabling has a 24h cooldown. New players are auto-protected for 48h. Spawn (500 block radius) is always PvP-free. Combat Tag locks logout/teleport/toggle for 15 seconds. Walling-in players where they can't free themselves is forbidden (see Server Rules).
