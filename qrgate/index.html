ABOUT QRGATE

Everything you need to run event ticketing.

QrGate is a complete web-based ticketing system for events of any size. Guests buy tickets through a clean online shop, receive a PDF with a unique QR code, and get scanned in at the door — no app required on either side.

Administrators get a live dashboard showing real-time ticket sales, revenue estimates, and event statistics. Box office staff can sell tickets on-site. Door staff use any smartphone to scan and validate QR codes instantly.

Active in production

QrGate powers the ticketing for Helenenbühne Sattledt — a parish theater group in Sattledt, Austria. Every season, ticket sales and door scanning run entirely through QrGate.

FEATURES

What QrGate does for you.

Online Ticket Shop

Guests visit your event page, pick their tickets, and pay — all in a browser. No account required. Works on desktop and mobile.

QR Code Check-In

Scan tickets at the door using any smartphone. Instant validation — valid, already used, or unknown.

Flexible Payment

Accept card payments via Stripe online, or handle cash sales at the box office — both managed in one system.

Automatic PDF Tickets

After purchase, guests receive a PDF ticket with a unique QR code by email — automatically, no manual steps. Downloadable any time.

Admin Dashboard

Live overview of sold and remaining tickets, revenue, and event stats. Manage dates, upload images, and configure your event — all in one place.

German & English

Full interface available in German and English — for your guests and your team.

HOW IT WORKS

From setup to sold-out.

Create your event

Set up your event in the admin panel — add dates, ticket prices, and event details.

Share the shop link

Point your guests to the ticket shop URL. They pick their tickets, fill in their info, and pay.

Guests get their ticket

A PDF ticket with a unique QR code lands in their inbox automatically right after purchase.

Scan at the door

Door staff open the handheld interface on any phone and scan QR codes. Valid or invalid — instant answer.

USER ROLES

The right access for everyone.

Admin

Full access

Manages the entire system: events, dates, ticket settings, revenue stats, and user access. Has access to all sections.

Ticketflow

Box office

For box office staff. Sells tickets on-site for cash or card. No access to admin settings or statistics.

Handheld

Door scanner

Mobile-optimized view for door staff. Scan QR codes and see immediate feedback — valid, already used, or not found. No other access.
